Welcome to Lux

A pioneering consulting firm incorporated in Aruba on December 1, 2023. Our mission is to revolutionize the financial landscape by leveraging cutting-edge technology and innovative solutions to empower businesses.

At Lux, we envision a world where financial services are seamless, efficient, and accessible to all. We are committed to driving financial inclusion and fostering economic growth through our state-of-the-art fintech solutions and accountancy services.

Services

We offer a comprehensive suite of services in accountancy, advisory, and fintech tailored to meet our clients' unique needs. Our team of seasoned professionals leverages cutting-edge technology and industry best practices to deliver unparalleled solutions. Whether you need precise financial reporting, strategic business advice, or advanced fintech solutions, we are here to empower your financial success.

Fintech

Process automation
We specialize in automating financial processes to increase efficiency and reduce operational costs. Our automation solutions are powered by AI and Python, ensuring seamless integration and optimal performance. Our solutions result in streamlined workflows, reduced manual intervention, Increased accuracy and consistency in financial operations, and significant time and cost savings.

Credit risk modeling
Our advanced credit risk modeling services ensure compliance with IFRS 9 standards. We leverage cutting-edge AI and machine learning algorithms to accurately assess and predict credit risk. Our solutions enhance credit risk assessment accuracy and improve financial reporting and transparency.

Accountancy

We offer a comprehensive range of services designed to meet businesses' evolving needs, delivering accurate, efficient, and insightful financial solutions to help businesses thrive.
